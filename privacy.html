Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. This policy explains how we collect, use, share and protect your information when you use SkillConnect.

1. Introduction

Welcome to SkillConnect (“we”, “us”, “our”). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website, register for an account, enroll in courses, or otherwise interact with our services (collectively, the “Services”).


2. Scope

This policy applies to all users of our Services, including learners, instructors, mentors, and corporate users. It explains your rights, our obligations, and how your data is handled responsibly.


3. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email, password, profile image.
  • Course Data: Enrollments, progress, certificates, and feedback.
  • Payment Data: Billing details (handled by secure third-party processors).
  • Device & Usage Data: Browser type, IP address, analytics, cookies.
  • Communications: Messages sent via our platform or support team.
  • Verification Data: Optional identity information for credentialed programs.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, operate, and personalize our Services.
  • Process course registrations and certifications.
  • Analyze platform usage for improvements.
  • Send transactional or promotional updates (if you’ve consented).
  • Maintain platform security and prevent fraud.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

5. Legal Bases for Processing

  • Contractual necessity – to deliver our Services.
  • Legal compliance – to satisfy applicable laws.
  • Legitimate interest – to improve and secure our platform.
  • Consent – for optional communications or data uses.

6. Sharing & Disclosure

  • Service Providers: For hosting, payments, analytics, communication.
  • Institutional Partners: For course administration and credentialing.
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law or governmental request.
  • Business Transfers: If SkillConnect merges, sells assets, or reorganizes.

7. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to enable essential site functionality, analyze traffic, and personalize content. You may adjust your cookie preferences in your browser or through our cookie settings tool.


8. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary for legitimate business purposes or legal requirements. You can request deletion of your data anytime via privacy@skillconnect.com.


9. Your Rights

  • Access and obtain a copy of your personal data.
  • Request correction or deletion of inaccurate data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time.
  • Object to data processing for certain purposes.

10. Children’s Privacy

Our Services are not intended for children under 13. If we discover personal information collected from a child without verified parental consent, we will promptly delete it.


11. Data Security

We use encryption, secure hosting, and access controls to protect your information. However, no digital transmission is 100% secure, and you use our Services at your own risk.


12. International Data Transfers

We may process data in countries outside your own. When we do, we apply appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or equivalent frameworks to ensure adequate protection.


13. Retention & Deletion

We keep your information for as long as necessary to deliver our Services or meet legal obligations. You can request deletion by contacting privacy@skillconnect.com.


14. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices or content. Please review their policies separately.


15. Region-Specific Notices

We comply with privacy laws in multiple jurisdictions. Depending on where you live, additional rights and disclosures may apply.


15.1 European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom & Switzerland (GDPR/UK GDPR)

If you reside in these regions, you have specific rights under the GDPR/UK GDPR.

  • Access, rectify, or erase your data.
  • Restrict or object to processing.
  • Data portability to another service.
  • Withdraw consent at any time.

We use lawful bases such as contractual necessity, legitimate interest, legal obligation, and consent. International transfers are protected using Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent safeguards.


15.2 California (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA):

  • Right to Know what data we collect and how it’s used.
  • Right to Delete your personal information.
  • Right to Correct inaccurate data.
  • Right to Opt Out of sale or sharing of personal data (we do not sell your data).
  • Right to Non-Discrimination for exercising your rights.

You can exercise your rights by contacting privacy@skillconnect.com.


15.3 Other Regions

We respect privacy laws globally. Users in Canada (PIPEDA), Brazil (LGPD), Singapore (PDPA), and Australia (Privacy Act) may have additional rights to access, correct, and delete their data.


16. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last Updated” date will always reflect the most recent version. We encourage you to review this page periodically.


17. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact:

Email: privacy@skillconnect.com
Address: SkillConnect Ltd., 123 Learning Ave, City, State, Country
